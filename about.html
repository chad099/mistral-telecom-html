About Us

MISTRAL TELECOM is leading Value-added Reseller of Canada.

MISTRAL TELECOM is leading Value-added Reseller of Canada’s top telecom service providers specializing in selling High Speed Unlimited Residential/Business Internet, International Long Distance and Business Phones in Ontario. We have partnered with best ISPs in Canada to provide the right solution to our customers.

Mistral was founded in 2018 by young and dynamic entrepreneurs which bring forth a combined total of over 10 years experience in the largest Telecom organisations operating in Ontario with direct knowledge of the industry with extensive sales and management skills. We have a strong expertise to grow company presence in the Residential Consumer Segment having delivered high quality results with our successful business model.
